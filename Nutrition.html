Why Good Nutrition Matters

Eating well is fundamental to health and well-being. Proper nutrition provides energy and supports physical and mental health.

Components of a Balanced Diet

  • Fruits and Vegetables: Rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Protein: Supports muscle and tissue repair
  • Whole Grains: Great source of fiber and nutrients
  • Healthy Fats: Important for brain health

Healthy Eating Tips

Make small changes for a healthier diet:

  • Choose water over sugary drinks
  • Include a variety of foods in each meal
  • Eat mindfully and listen to hunger cues
  • Plan meals ahead to avoid last-minute choices
